Our Mission

We focus on three pillars for sustainable development in Africa:

Training in African Schools

Our agroecological training programs empower students and farmers to become self-sufficient.

Development of a Training Center in Bamako

Providing hands-on, sustainable agriculture techniques to ensure long-term productivity.

Development of a Seed Bank

Ensuring biodiversity and resilience through access to locally adapted seeds.
